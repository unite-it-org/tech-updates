Tech Updates - June 2025

69% of Tech Leaders Plan to Expand Teams with GenAI

A Deloitte survey shows most CIOs/CTOs expect generative AI to **create** new roles—driving demand for AI/ML engineers, prompt engineers, and cybersecurity experts. Soft skills plus AI expertise are key ().

AI Revolutionizes Indian BPOs, But Entry Jobs at Risk

Leading firms now deploy real-time AI co‑pilots and accent‑altering tools in call centers. This boosts efficiency but threatens frontline roles—prompting urgent reskilling efforts ().

Rajasthan Launches AI Centre of Excellence in Agriculture

State govt initiates CoE to train youth in AI-driven agri‑tech—boosting jobs and innovation across farming and industrial sectors ().

Indian Global Capability Centres Taking on AI & R&D Leadership

More GCCs are shifting to high-value roles in AI-led R&D and innovation, thanks to India’s talent pool—marking a strategic upgrade from service-based functions ().

IndiaAI Mission Launches Shared GPU Infrastructure

A new cluster offering ~10,000 GPUs (AMD & Nvidia) is now live—supporting startups, researchers, and public-sector AI innovation across healthcare, agri, disaster relief ().

Ola‑backed Krutrim Launches Multilingual AI Agent “Kruti”

Released June 12, Kruti performs tasks like booking taxis and ordering food across 13 Indian languages. It uses agents to break tasks down and aims for broader integration with apps like Swiggy, Uber ().
